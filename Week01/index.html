Hi there! My name is Anika, and I am majoring in Studio Art and Emerging Media, with a minor in Sociology. I have a strong interest in interdisciplinary studies and am eager to learn more about web design!


Statement

In my work I explore absurdist themes in popular media. Much of my work is motivated by the junction of art and media, specifically how media can be used as a medium of art and art production. I am interested in the role of media in the social construction of race, gender and religion; as well as the impact it has had on my own psyche. My work invites questions on human existence and the mysteries of our psyche, drawing from the tapestries of both absurdism and surrealism. By de-voiding things of meaning, I hope to redefine the values we hold within everyday life. My work is a combination of design, printmaking, film and illustration.
